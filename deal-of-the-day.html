How TripHunt finds the Deal of the Day

Every morning, our system scans thousands of routes from UK airports and scores each fare against the typical price for that route. We look for flights that are at least 25% below the average — that's what earns the "Deal of the Day" label.

Prices are sourced live from Aviasales, one of the largest flight search engines in the world. All fares shown are return flights per person, and you book directly with the airline or OTA — TripHunt never charges a booking fee.

The deal refreshes daily at 8am UTC. Prices can change quickly, so if you see a deal you like, book soon.
