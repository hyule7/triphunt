How error fares work

Airlines occasionally misprice flights due to currency conversion errors, fuel surcharge omissions, or system glitches. These fares are usually 40–90% below normal price and can last anywhere from minutes to a few hours. Airlines are legally required to honour them in most cases. Book fast, book flexible.
